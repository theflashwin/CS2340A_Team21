About

Healthy Habits

Build Healthy and Sustainable Habits

GreenPlate is committed to encouraging environmentally friendly eating and shopping practices. The design of the app is centered on encouraging resource efficiency and minimizing food waste, allowing users to participate actively in a more environmentally friendly way of living. With the Food Tracker app, users can make shopping lists, manage ingredient proportions, develop recipes, and keep track of their daily caloric consumption. The app provides users with useful information about recipes, ingredients, and what they need to buy in order to prepare future meals.
